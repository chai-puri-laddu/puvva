Tulsi (Holy Basil)

Scientific Name: Ocimum tenuiflorum

Holy Basil or Tulsi is a sacred plant in Hindu belief, native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. It's used in Ayurveda and traditional medicine for its adaptogenic and healing properties.

Hibiscus

Scientific Name: Hibiscus rosa-sinensis

Hibiscus is known for its large, colorful flowers and is often used in herbal teas and traditional medicine. It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is also called Roselle in some places.
